Tagineer is a natural-languaging processing application written by Josh Weinstein. It can tag the parts of speech in English words.

The available tags are:

Noun-S(singular), Noun-P(plural)

Verbs, 

Adjectives,

Adverbs,

Propositions,

Coordinating Conjunctions,

Subordinating Conjunctions
